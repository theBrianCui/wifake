Enter UT EID

If you have forgotten your password or received a message that your UT EID is locked, you can use this procedure to reset it.

NOTE: After changing your EID password, you must immediately update any applications which authenticate using your UT EID and EID password on all devices (work/home computer, smartphone, and tablet) to avoid being locked out of your accounts. This includes email accounts, calendaring, and the credentials used to connect to the UT wireless network, if you do so. (You do not have to update UTMail accounts which use domain "@utexas.edu".)
